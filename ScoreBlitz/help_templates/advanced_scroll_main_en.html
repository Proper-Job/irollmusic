Roll by Measure Help

The Roll by Measure performance style gives you fine grained control over how your score plays back.

In order to get started with the Roll by Measure performance style use the Editor to add Measures to your score. Tap the button in the bottom left corner to start the Editor.

Once you have entered Measures in the Editor you can use the playback controls to start and stop the performance.

Tap on any toolbar button to get more information on the functions that it performs.

To show or hide the toolbars touch the screen with two fingers.
