Manual Help

The Manual performance style allows you to work with one page at a time. Swipe one finger to navigate between pages.

The screen is split along the middle into two Tap Zones. They are active parts of the screen that respond to taps with a single finger.

Pressing the Right Tap Zone advances the score while pressing the Left Tap Zone rewinds the score. If you're working in landscape orientation, the Tap Zones advance and rewind by half a page.

You can enable or disable the Tap Zones from within the App Settings.

Tap on any toolbar button to get more information on the functions that it performs.

To show or hide the toolbars touch the screen with two fingers.
