Signs
This annotation style let's you add fingerings, dynamics, ornaments and articulations into your scores.
The buttons on the left give you quick access to the most recently used annotations. Tap on any item in this list to quickly insert it into your score.
The slider allows you to precisely adjust the size of the selected annotation.
The reset button resets the annotation to its original size.
Use two fingers to select and edit multiple annotations.
Long press and drag an annotation to move it to a new location.
You can manually resize an annotation by simply dragging out its bottom right edge.
